Uno Card Instructions

Starting the game

Each player starts with 7 cards. The player who made the lobby goes first, and players play in sequential order.

On Your Turn

  • You may play a card from your hand if it matches the color or number of the card played by the last player
  • You may draw a card from the deck if you cannot play (or if you just want to)
  • See the card descriptions for special effects they may affect what you can play on your turn

Winning and Losing the Game

  • You win the game by playing all of your cards
  • If you have more than 16 cards at any time, you are knocked out
  • When someone wins, the remaining players are ranked by their number of cards
  • Ties are broken by turn order

The Attack Count

Whenever a draw 2, draw 3, or draw 5 card is played, the attack count is increased. The next player can either draw the number of cards in the attack count or play an attack card of greater value (Specifically, draw 2 cannot be played on draw 3, and nothing can be played on draw 5). Drawing resets the attack count to 0. The Shield card can be used to block any attack.

Card descriptions

Card Description
Number cards are basic and have no additional effect.
The Skip card causes the next player's turn to be skipped, meaning they do not get to play a card this round.
The Reverse card causes the direction of play to be reversed.
The One More card lets you play an additional card before the next player's turn.
A wild card lets you change the color from the color of the wild card, to a different (or the same) color. Note that the wild card can only be played on top of cards of its specific color or other wild cards.
The Gift card gives all other players 2 cards.
The Trash card discards all green cards from players hands, and changes the color.
The Shield card blocks all attacks, meaning you don't have to pick up any cards. It can be played on any card.
The Pass card skips your turn, passing the attack count to the next player. It can be played on any card.
The Draw Two card increases the Attack Count by 2.
The Draw Three card increases the Attack Count by 3.
The Draw Five card increases the Attack Count by 5.
